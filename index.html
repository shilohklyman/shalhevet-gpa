Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Information We Collect

  • •Account information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google solely to identify your account.
  • •Academic data: GPA entries, course names, grades, credits, tasks, college list entries, and co-curricular activities that you manually enter.
  • •Transcript data (optional): If you use the PDF import feature, your transcript is processed to extract course names, grades, and credits only.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to power the features of the Dashboard — displaying your GPA, tracking tasks, and syncing your data across devices when signed in. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

Data Storage & Security

  • •Signed-in users: Data is stored in Google Firebase/Firestore, which is protected by Google's security infrastructure.
  • •Guest users: Data is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to any server.
  • •We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.

Third-Party Services

The Dashboard relies on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • •Google Firebase: Used for authentication and cloud data storage.
  • •OpenAI: Used only when you choose to import a PDF transcript. Your transcript is transmitted to OpenAI for processing and is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You may delete your data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in the GPA Calculator. Guest users can clear their data by clearing their browser's local storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated date. Continued use of the Dashboard after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

HOW IT WORKS

  • •Unweighted (REG): Standard 4.0 scale. All courses calculated equally.
  • •Weighted (WT): SAS and Honors courses receive a +1.0 bonus point.
  • •Advanced Track: Unweighted per Shalhevet policy — no bonus point applied.
  • •Auto-Weighting: Course names with "SAS" or "Honors" auto-toggle to be weighted.
  • •Credits: Most courses = 0.5 credits per semester.
  • •In-Progress Courses: Leaving a grade as "—" keeps the course on your transcript draft without counting it in GPA totals yet.
  • •Sync S2 Toggle: Mirrors semester 1 course into semester 2. Only the Semester 2 grade stays editable.
  • •Transcript Export: Generates an unofficial transcript from the grades you've entered, organized by year. Not an official school document.
  • •PDF Import: Sends your transcript to OpenAI for processing. Only courses and grades are extracted. No personal data is stored.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • •Account information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google solely to identify your account.
  • •Academic data: GPA entries, course names, grades, credits, tasks, college list entries, and co-curricular activities that you manually enter. This data is private to your account by default; other students/users cannot view it.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to power the features of the Dashboard — displaying your GPA, tracking tasks, and syncing your data across devices when signed in. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

Data Storage & Security

  • •Signed-in users: Data is stored in Google Firebase/Firestore and tied to your user account for private access and sync.
  • •Guest users: Data is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to any server.
  • •Confidentiality: We do not sell, rent, or share your academic data for commercial purposes, and we do not operate any public data feed or student-visible database.
  • •Access controls: Student records are intended to be visible only to the signed-in account owner inside the app experience.
  • •Important technical note: Like most cloud apps, authorized project administrators and cloud providers may have limited technical access when required for maintenance, reliability, or security. No intentional manual review of student academic records is part of normal operation.

Third-Party Services

The Dashboard relies on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • •Google Firebase: Used for authentication and cloud data storage.
  • •OpenAI: Used as a secondary step during PDF transcript import, only when the local parser produces an incomplete result. Personal fields are redacted before transmission. Only redacted academic text (course names, grades, credits) is sent, and it is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You may delete your data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in the GPA Tracker. Guest users can clear their data by clearing their browser's local storage.

HOW IT WORKS

  • •Unweighted (REG): Standard 4.0 scale. All courses calculated equally.
  • •Weighted (WT): SAS and Honors courses receive a +1.0 bonus point.
  • •Advanced Track: Unweighted per Shalhevet policy — no bonus point applied.
  • •Auto-Weighting: Course names with "SAS" or "Honors" auto-toggle to be weighted.
  • •Credits: Most courses = 0.5 credits per semester.
  • •Sync S2 Toggle: Mirrors semester 1 course into semester 2. Only the Semester 2 grade stays editable.
  • •Pass / Withdraw / No Grade: Grades of Pass (P), Withdraw (W), or No Grade (NG) are not factored into GPA.
  • •Unofficial Transcript Export: Generates a transcript from the grades you've entered, organized by year. Not an official school document.
  • •PDF Import: Your transcript is parsed locally first — no data leaves your device unless the local result is incomplete, in which case redacted academic text is sent to OpenAI for a second pass. Only courses and grades are extracted. No personal data is stored.

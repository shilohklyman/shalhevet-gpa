HOW IT WORKS

  • •Unweighted (REG): Standard 4.0 scale. All courses calculated equally.
  • •Weighted (WT): SAS and Honors courses receive a +1.0 bonus point.
  • •Advanced Track: Unweighted per Shalhevet policy — no bonus point applied.
  • •Auto-Weighting: Course names with "SAS" or "Honors" auto-toggle to WT.
  • •Credits: Most courses = 0.5 credits per semester.
  • •Sync S2 Toggle: The slide toggle on each S1 course mirrors that course into semester 2 — copying the name, credits, and weight. Only the S2 grade stays editable. Use this for full-year courses you take both semesters.
